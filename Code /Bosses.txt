==============================
         Delta Sphinx
==============================

An attempt to recreate the legendary Sphinx,  
combining telepathy and riddles.  
The experiment spiraled out of control, leaving  
a fractured mind and shifting form.


==============================
          Harpy-X
==============================

Inspired by the mythic harpies, this failed experiment  
added mechanical wings and amplified vocal organs.  
The result is a screeching, erratic predator beyond control.


==============================
        Anubis-Void
==============================

A twisted attempt to capture the essence of Anubis.  
Dark energies fused with the experiment’s body,  
creating a hollow, void-filled guardian gone rogue.


==============================
        Zeus-Cracked
==============================

Modeled after the god of thunder, but the process failed.  
Electricity surged uncontrollably through his malformed body,  
turning him into a walking storm of raw energy.


==============================
           Hydra-13
==============================

Inspired by the multi-headed Hydra,  
the experiment fused multiple animal traits and radiation.  
When damaged, it regenerates new malformed heads uncontrollably.


==============================
     The Broken Minotaur
==============================

A reconstruction of the Minotaur myth gone horribly wrong.  
Its massive body is twisted and unstable,  
and its rage-infused charge strikes fear into all who face it.


==============================
            Medusa
==============================

An experiment meant to replicate the gorgon’s gaze.  
Metallic tendrils replaced snakes,  
and its petrifying power became unstable and unpredictable.


==============================
       Cerberus-Voidspawn
==============================

A failed recreation of Cerberus,  
with one skull and three misaligned jaws.  
The experiment went beyond control, turning it into a chaotic predator.


==============================
         Chimera-Blight
==============================

Modeled after the mythical Chimera,  
stitched together from various animals.  
Each mutation amplified uncontrollably, creating elemental chaos.


==============================
        Typhon-Radialis
==============================

An ambitious attempt to recreate Typhon.  
Massive, unstable limbs and volatile energy make  
it a mutation that defies control or containment.
